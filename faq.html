Frequently Asked Questions

What are your operating hours?
We are open from 10 AM to 10 PM every day.
Do you have vegetarian / vegan options?
Yes, we have a variety of vegetarian and vegan dishes available.
Do you offer catering services for events?
Yes, we offer catering services for various events. Please contact us for more details.
What is your cancellation policy for reservations?
We require at least 24 hours' notice for reservation cancellations.
Is your food halal?
Yes, all our dishes are prepared in accordance with halal guidelines.
Are there any special discounts or promotions?
Yes, we have various promotions throughout the year. Please check our website or follow us on social media for the latest deals.
Can I make a reservation online?
Yes, you can make a reservation through our website or by calling us directly.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash, credit cards, and mobile payments such as e-wallets.
Do you have Wi-Fi available for customers?
Yes, we offer free Wi-Fi for our customers.
Is your restaurant wheelchair accessible?
Yes, our restaurant is wheelchair accessible.
How can I provide feedback about my experience?
We welcome your feedback. You can provide it through our contact form on our website, DM-ing our social medias, or tell it directly to our staff.
