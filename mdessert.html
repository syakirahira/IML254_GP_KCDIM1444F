Malay dessert

RM5

Kuih lapis

A traditional layered steamed cake, each layer having a different color and flavor, usually made with coconut milk, rice flour, and tapioca flour.

RM4

Ais kacang

Also known as ABC (Air Batu Campur), it is a shaved ice dessert topped with various colorful ingredients such as red beans, sweet corn, grass jelly, and colorful syrups.

RM4

Kek batik

A Malaysian no-bake cake made from crushed Marie biscuits, cocoa powder, condensed milk, and sometimes butter, creating a rich and chocolatey treat.

RM4

Kek pisang

A moist banana cake made with ripe bananas, flour, eggs, sugar, and butter, often enjoyed as a tea-time snack or dessert.

RM4

Kek pandan

A pandan-flavored sponge cake, light and fluffy with a hint of pandan aroma, commonly served on special occasions or as a treat with a cup of tea or coffee.
